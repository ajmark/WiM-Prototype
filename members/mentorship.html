Mentorship


To achieve our goal of retaining women physicians, we offer a mentoring program through WiM. Residents and junior physicians are paired with senior level physicians to offer different perspectives on how to find success in medicine today. It is a great networking experience and support system. Mentees are advised on work/life balance, how to climb the ladder, negotiation skills, and confrontation.
