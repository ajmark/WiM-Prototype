Community Service

Pumpkinfest

Date: October 12, 2013

Time: 12:00am-4:00pm

Location: 4th Floor Cancer Center, Prostate Conference Rm. 463

Description:
The East Allegheny Community Council is hosting the annual Pumpkinfest in Deutchtown. For months ahead kids look forward to Pumpkinfest in October. Where else can kids see puppet shows, get pony rides, pet a llama, bob for apples, eat funnel cake, win cool prizes, and get a free health checkup all in one afternoon?! This popular Fall festival for kids draws between 600 and 1200 children and parents each year. The doctors in WiM volunteer to give these free health checkups.

Fun, Fit, and Fabulous Women's Conference

Date: August 17, 2013

Time: 10:00am-5:00pm

Location: Wyndham Grand in Downtown Pittsburgh

Description:
Over the last 10 years; the Fun, Fit, and Fabulous Women's Conference has provided more than 10,000 women with access to dynamic keynote speakers, impressive workshops presenters and fun activities that show just how easy living a healthy lifestyle can be. Through workshops focused on prominent health issues, the conference has educated women about such things as the importance of physical activity and healthy cooking, cancer prevention, diabetes and heart disease. Members of WiM volunteer their time by hosting workshops and giving free health screenings at the conference.
